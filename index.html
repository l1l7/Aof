About Ace of Fades

Ace of Fades is a top-rated barbershop in Twentynine Palms known for skilled barbers, chill desert vibes, and next-level service. With free WiFi, a pool table, and complimentary beer and popcorn, every visit feels like home. We specialize in fades and straight razor shaves for men, women, and kids of all hair types.
