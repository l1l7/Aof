About Ace of Fades

Ace of Fades is a popular barbershop in Twentynine Palms known for its skilled barbers, welcoming atmosphere, and amenities like free WiFi, a pool table, and complimentary drinks. They offer a full range of services for men, women, and kids, specializing in fades and straight razor shaves.

Services

  • ✔ Classic Haircuts & Custom Cuts
  • ✔ Skin, Low, Mid, High & Drop Fades
  • ✔ Beard Trims & Maintenance
  • ✔ Straight Razor Shaves
  • ✔ Hair Coloring
  • ✔ Kids, Women’s & Military Haircuts
  • ✔ Hot Towel Shaves & Shampoo
  • ✔ Waxing & Eyebrow Trimming
